About Us

At Cars247.ng, we believe in more than just selling cars; we're committed to creating memorable experiences and building lasting relationships with our valued customers. Nestled in the heart of Lagos, Nigeria, we pride ourselves on being a premier destination for quality vehicles and exceptional customer service

Established in 2013, Cars247.ng has been a cornerstone of the automotive industry in Nigeria for 10+ years. What started as a humble endeavor has blossomed into a thriving dealership known for its integrity, transparency, and dedication to customer satisfaction. Over the years, we've served countless customers, each with unique needs and preferences, and it's been our privilege to match them with their dream cars.

At Cars247.ng, we understand that purchasing a vehicle is a significant investment, which is why we're dedicated to providing you with a seamless and stress-free buying experience. Our knowledgeable sales team is here to guide you through every step of the process, from exploring our extensive inventory to securing competitive financing options. We're committed to transparency and honesty, ensuring that you have all the information you need to make an informed decision.
